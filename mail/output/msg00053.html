When Andres Amarilla was 17, he partnered with Geraldine Rojas (then
age 13) for a year.  Together, they performed over 20 times at Buenos
Aires milongas, including Sunderland, Akarence, and Sin Rumbo.

After a lot of searching, we were finally able to locate a video of
one of Andres and Geraldine's performances.  This one was given in
October 1993 at the milonga Glorias Argentinas as an homage to the
legendary Petroleo.  You can see Petroleo at the end of the video,
standing behind the table in the center of the frame.

Take a look:
http://andresamarilla.com/video.htm
